Aperol Spritz

Servings: 1
Calories: 100cal
Ready in: 5min
Good for: Party

  • Ingredients

  • 2 oz. Aperol
  • 3 oz. prosecco
  • 2 oz. (or a splash, or a top off) club soda
  • orange slices

Instructions

Build Aperol, prosecco, and club soda in a wine glass over ice. Garnish with fresh orange slices in the glass.
